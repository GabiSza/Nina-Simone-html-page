Nina Simone was an American singer, songwriter, musician, and civil rights activist. Her music spanned a broad range of musical styles including classical, jazz, blues, folk, R&B, gospel, and pop. She is known for her powerful and soulful voice and her lyrics often dealt with themes of social injustice and civil rights.
